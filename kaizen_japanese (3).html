The Ultimate Beginner's Roadmap to Japan

Step-by-step milestones to direct your early learning. Tap the checkboxes below to track your study progress.

Roadmap Completed
0% Done
01
Milestone 1: Character Foundations

Master Basic Hiragana & Katakana

Avoid relying on romaji spelling early on. Learn the native characters (46 core Hiragana, then 46 Katakana) using visual memory cues and stroke guides.

02
Milestone 2: Sentence Grammar Structure

Learn the SOV Structure & Simple Particles

Japanese uses Subject-Object-Verb (SOV) order. Focus on how particles like は (wa), が (ga), and を (o) function as grammatical glue.

03
Milestone 3: Pronunciation & Flow

Listen to High vs Low Pitch-Accents

Japanese words rise and fall in pitch. Focus on learning words with their correct pitch accent early on to develop natural, clean speech patterns.
